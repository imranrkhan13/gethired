# gethired

From job description → interview-ready resume.

## Uses

- Turn any job post into a resume that actually speaks that company’s language  
- Stop guessing what to write — align your experience with what they’re hiring for  
- Replace generic bullets with sharp, role-specific impact  
- Cut the noise → force everything into a clean, one-page story  
- Apply to multiple roles without rewriting your resume from scratch  
- Beat ATS filters by matching real keywords, not stuffing  
- Stay honest — no fake metrics, just better framing of your actual work  
- Ship resumes faster than you can overthink them
